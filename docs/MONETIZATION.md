# Monetization Strategy: BudgetPilot

## Pricing Model

Freemium with Premium subscription.

## Free Plan

- Manual transactions
- Basic monthly summary
- Limited categories

## Premium Plan

- Unlimited categories and budgets
- Advanced analytics and trend insights
- Shared family budget
- Recurring transactions
- Data export and backup

## Proposed Pricing

- Monthly: $3.99
- Yearly: $29.99

## Revenue Expansion

- Partner offers for financial products
- White-label B2B version for coaching programs
- Premium coaching templates marketplace

## KPI Targets

- Free-to-paid conversion: 3-6% in first 6 months
- MRR growth: 15% month-over-month after launch
